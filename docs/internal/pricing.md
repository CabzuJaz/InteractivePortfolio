## Rate Range: $10–15/hour

### Complexity Tiers

| Complexity | Rate | Examples |
|------------|------|----------|
| Simple | $10/hr | Basic n8n workflow, single integration, simple automation |
| Moderate | $12/hr | Multi-step workflows, API integrations, GHL automation |
| Complex | $15/hr | AI/ML systems, custom agents, multi-agent pipelines |

### Client Type Adjustments

| Client Type | Adjustment | Effective Range |
|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| Startup | -$2/hr | $8–13/hr (min $10) |
| Small Business | Base rate | $10–15/hr |
| Enterprise | +$3/hr | $13–18/hr (max $15) |

### Hour Buffer

All estimates include a **+24 hour buffer** for:
- Project management
- Testing and QA
- Client communication
- Handover and documentation

### Examples

| Scenario | Hours | Rate | Labor | Tools | Total |
|----------|-------|------|-------|-------|-------|
| Simple n8n for startup | 40+24=64 | $10 | $640 | $185 | $825 |
| GHL automation for small biz | 60+24=84 | $12 | $1,008 | $185 | $1,193 |
| Custom AI for enterprise | 80+24=104 | $15 | $1,560 | $370 | $1,930 |
