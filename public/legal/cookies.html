Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how Build with Jazz, operated by Jazzmin Sicat-Cabizares ("we", "us", or "our"), uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and services (the "Services"). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies, such as local storage and pixels, store or read information on your device. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used.

3. Types of cookies we use

Category Purpose
Strictly necessary Required for the Services to function, including authentication via Facebook Login, security, and load balancing. These cannot be switched off in our systems.
Preference Remember choices you make, such as language or display settings, to improve your experience.
Analytics Help us understand how the Services are used so we can maintain and improve them. Where required, these are set only with your consent.

4. Third-party cookies

When you authenticate through Facebook Login or otherwise interact with Meta platforms, Meta may set its own cookies, which are governed by Meta's own policies. We do not control cookies set by third parties. Please review the relevant third party's cookie and privacy policies for details.

5. How to manage cookies

Most browsers let you view, block, and delete cookies through their settings. You can also set your browser to notify you when cookies are being set. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect the availability and functionality of the Services.

Where required by applicable law, we request your consent before setting non-essential cookies, and you can withdraw that consent at any time.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
