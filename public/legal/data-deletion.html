Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Overview

Build with Jazz, operated by Jazzmin Sicat-Cabizares, respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of the personal information we hold about you, including data obtained through Facebook Login, Messenger, and the Graph API.

2. How to request deletion

You can request that we delete your personal data using either method below.

Option A — Email request

  1. Send an email to jazzmincabizares@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include the name and, where available, the email address or account identifier associated with your use of our Services, so we can locate your data.
  3. We will confirm receipt and process your request, as described in section 3 below.

Option B — Remove the app on Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy → Settings.
  2. Select Apps and Websites.
  3. Locate Build with Jazz in the list of connected apps.
  4. Select the app and choose Remove.

Removing the app revokes our access to your Facebook data going forward. To also have data we have already stored deleted, please also submit an email request using Option A.

3. What happens after you request deletion

  • We verify the request to protect against unauthorized deletion.
  • We delete the personal data associated with your account from our active systems, and from backups in the ordinary course of our backup rotation.
  • We confirm completion by email. We aim to complete verified requests within 30 days.

We may retain limited information where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud and abuse, or enforce our agreements. Any retained data is protected and used only for those purposes.

4. Related information

For more detail on what data we collect and how it is used, see our Privacy Policy.

5. Contact

If you have questions about deleting your data, contact us at jazzmincabizares@gmail.com.
